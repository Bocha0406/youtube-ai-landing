1. Introduction

YouTube AI Agent ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Telegram bot and web services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from Telegram

  • Telegram user ID
  • Username (if provided)
  • First and last name (if provided)

2.2 Information from YouTube (via OAuth)

  • YouTube channel ID and name
  • Channel statistics (subscribers, views, video count)
  • Video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails)
  • Analytics data (traffic sources, demographics)
  • Comments on your videos

2.3 Usage Data

  • Bot commands and interactions
  • Features used and frequency
  • Timestamps of actions

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide YouTube channel analytics and insights
  • Generate reports and charts
  • Optimize SEO and content recommendations
  • Manage comments and community
  • Improve our service quality
  • Communicate important updates

4. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth Tokens: Stored encrypted in our secure database
  • Channel Data: Cached temporarily for performance, updated regularly
  • Reports: Stored locally on our server with access controls
  • Security: We use industry-standard encryption and security practices

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with third parties.

Your YouTube data is accessed only through official YouTube Data API and used exclusively to provide our services to you.

6. Your Rights and Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data we store about you
  • Disconnect: Revoke OAuth access at any time via /disconnect command or Google Account Settings
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as account is active
  • Disconnected channels: OAuth tokens deleted immediately
  • Inactive accounts: Data automatically deleted after 180 days of inactivity
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
